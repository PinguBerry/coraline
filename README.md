# 🐧 Hello, we are the Pinguberry Team!

We are participating in the **RoboCup Junior OnStage** in South Korea, and this project is dedicated to it.

Our team is entirely female, composed of two girls: **Cat and Gigi**.

We are an **independent team**, meaning we are not affiliated with any educational institution. All of our costs are self-funded.

Our goal with this repository is to share and document all the technologies we use in our project.

---

## About our project! 💜

Our presentation is based on the stop-motion movie **"Coraline"** and portrays Coraline's discovery of the *Other House*, a magical universe parallel to the real world.

It contains **4 robots**:
- a Coraline animatronic  
- a live-being piano  
- two static robots: the Portal and a Kitten  

In addition, there are **two software systems**:
- Image Recognition  
- Unity displayed on the screen  

---

## ✨ FEATURES ✨

- Coraline’s Electromechanical system 
- Unity as an extension of the stage  
- Image Recognition as an automation tool  
- Piano as a live being
